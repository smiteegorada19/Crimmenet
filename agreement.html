Filing a Complaint on National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal

This portal is an initiative to facilitate victims to report cyber crimes online. The portal focuses on cyber crimes, especially against women and children, and is handled by law enforcement agencies.

Please contact local police for emergencies or crimes other than cyber crimes. National police helpline: 112. Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930.
