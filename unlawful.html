Unlawful Content

Understanding, Identifying, and Reporting Unlawful Content

Unlawful content refers to any material that violates legal and ethical standards, including but not limited to:

  • Child exploitation and abuse materials
  • Hate speech, racism, or incitement to violence
  • Fraudulent schemes and scams
  • Cyberstalking, harassment, and bullying
  • Illegal drug trade, terrorism-related content

If you come across any unlawful content online, you can report it to the authorities through our platform. Your report will be reviewed, and necessary actions will be taken.
