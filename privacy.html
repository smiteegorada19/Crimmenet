Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Read our policy to understand how we handle your data.

1. Introduction

CrimeNet is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This policy details the personal data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and location data.
  • Incident Details: Reports, descriptions, and uploaded media files.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and operating systems.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Facilitating incident reporting and response coordination.
  • Enhancing platform security and user experience.
  • Communicating updates, alerts, or feedback requests.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Law enforcement agencies to assist in investigations.
  • Third-party service providers for data processing and storage.
  • Other users or community members in anonymous formats to foster safety awareness.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Withdraw consent for specific data processing activities.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, gather analytics, and ensure platform functionality. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Revised" date.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this policy, contact us at privacy@crimenet.com.
