About GAC

The Grievance Appellate Committee (GAC) deals with appeals from users (Digital Nagriks) aggrieved by decisions of Grievance Officers of social media intermediaries and other intermediaries. This includes complaints regarding violations of rules and other matters related to computer resources made available by the intermediary.

GAC is an online dispute resolution mechanism. The entire appeal process, from filing to decision, is digital. The Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021 ("IT Rules, 2021") and GAC aim to ensure a Safe, Trusted, and Accountable Internet for Indian users.

GAC functions as a regulatory body overseeing the resolution of appeals related to social media platforms, including but not limited to Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.

Note:

Cases/matters relating to financial fraud or cybercrime should be reported to the cybercrime helpline number 1930 or by visiting the website www.cybercrime.gov.in.

Before filing an appeal, please refer to the FAQ section on the GAC website: https://gac.gov.in/CMSData/FAQs for any assistance.

GAC does not handle financial fraud or cybercrime cases.
